Test Procedure

Six steps to a certified ethanol reading.

Using the 'NABL' Class Ethanol Testing Kit. Follows Petroleum Industry Specification.

1

Measure fuel sample

Take 100 ml of ethanol blended gasoline in a clean 150 ml measuring cylinder.

2

Add distilled water

Add exactly 30 ml of distilled water using the graduated pipette.

3

Shake vigorously

Insert the stopper tightly and shake the cylinder for 30 seconds.

4

Release pressure

Loosen the stopper to release vapor pressure — do not remove it.

5

Let it settle

Allow the cylinder to stand undisturbed for 5 minutes.

6

Read the meniscus

Observe the separation and read the aqueous layer at the bottom of the meniscus.

Formula
Aqueous layer (ml) = Volume of water in 100 ml sample − 30 ml

Product 02 · Field Bottle

EthoSure — For Ethanol Testing

Fast • Accurate • Certified. A pocket-sized bottle with a printed scale for on-site ethanol measurement in Ethanol Blended Motor Gasoline.

Objective

Determine ethanol content in EBMS by extracting ethanol into water and measuring the aqueous layer.

Principle

Ethanol has a greater affinity for water than gasoline. The increase in water volume is proportional to ethanol content.

Water Extraction Method

EthoSure — four steps in the field.

Instructions are printed directly on the bottle for on-site reference.

1

Fill water

Fill water to the water line.

2

Add fuel sample

Add fuel sample to the fuel line.

3

Shake & settle

Close the cap, shake 10–15 sec, let it settle for 2–3 minutes.

4

Read the scale

Read the ethanol percentage from the scale.

!
